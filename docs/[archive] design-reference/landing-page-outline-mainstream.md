**Q: Is this really free?**
A: Yes! No downloads, no credit card, no hidden fees. Just click and play.

**Q: What makes my pig unique?**
A: When you start, you get a unique pig with randomized color pattern, speed, and size. No two pigs are exactly alike!

**Q: How do I get a high score?**
A: Faster pigs collect coins more quickly. Look for golden coins (5x points)! You have 60 seconds per round.

**Q: Can I play on mobile?**
A: Currently optimized for desktop with keyboard controls. Mobile version coming soon!

**Q: Do I need to create an account?**
A: Just a quick signup - username and password. Takes 30 seconds.

**Q: What's the daily reset?**
A: Coin positions reset every day at midnight. Everyone gets the same layout - fair competition!

## Key Changes

1. **Remove/Minimize:**
   - Starknet, Cairo, Cartridge mentions
   - VRF, on-chain, blockchain terminology
   - Wallet, transaction, gas fee discussions
   - Contract addresses, explorer links

2. **Emphasize:**
   - Game mechanics (60s rounds, coins, patterns)
   - Competition (leaderboard, achievements)
   - Accessibility (free, browser-based)
   - Fun and visual appeal

3. **Keep Subtle (if needed):**
   - "Powered by Starknet" in small footer
   - "Built for ETHGlobal" in credits
   - Tech stack in a "Learn More" expandable section
